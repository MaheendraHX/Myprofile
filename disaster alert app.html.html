About the Platform

The Disaster Alert Command Center allows authorities to instantly broadcast life-saving warnings using cell broadcast technology. The system works even when the internet fails, ensuring that emergency information reaches citizens during earthquakes, floods, tsunamis, and other disasters.

Alerts are transmitted directly through cellular towers and received by every powered-on phone in the target area. No mobile apps, registration, or data connection is required.

This platform is designed for national disaster agencies, emergency services, and governments to ensure fast, reliable communication during crises.
